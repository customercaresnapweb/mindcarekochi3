Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our psychiatric and counselling services.

Is online psychiatric consultation available across India?

Yes, we provide highly secure telepsychiatry and online psychiatric consultations for patients anywhere in India from the comfort of their homes.

Are psychiatric consultations confidential?

Absolutely. Patient confidentiality is our highest priority. Everything discussed during your consultation remains strictly private between you and the doctor.

Can I receive prescription PDFs online?

Yes. Following an online consultation, if medication is deemed medically necessary and appropriate, a valid digital prescription in PDF format will be provided securely.

Is psychotherapy and counselling available online?

Yes, we offer supportive psychotherapy, stress counselling, and relationship counselling both in-person at our Kochi clinic and online across India.

Is help available for relationship and sexual health concerns?

Yes, we provide a stigma-free, highly professional environment for sex therapy and relationship counselling. Stress and anxiety heavily affect sexual health, and we provide comprehensive treatments including for erectile dysfunction and performance anxiety.

Are EFT tapping and mindfulness sessions available?

Yes, we offer specialized EFT (Emotional Freedom Techniques) tapping classes and mindfulness guidance designed to promote deep stress reduction and emotional awareness.
